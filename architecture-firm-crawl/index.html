Architecture Firm Crawl

Thursday, April 6 from 12:30 – 5:00pm after meeting at Vault Virginia

Tour local architecture firms and workspaces.

Take a guided tour through Charlottesville's downtown architecture and interior design firms. Visit the agencies’ offices, hear about their work, and see the available creative opportunities.

Destinations

    Brown Reynolds Watford Architects designs and builds the places where people serve, connect, learn, and thrive.

    The mission at BDA is to improve nature and culture through design.

    See their work

    Design Develop's mission is to bring a client’s vision for a project to life using photorealistic rendering, three-dimensional modeling, and virtual reality, fostering collaboration with our team from the earliest stages of design.

    Waterstreet Studio is a multi-disciplinary environmental design firm whose primary objective is to connect people to place, creating purposeful and engaging landscapes that are both economically viable and environmentally responsible.
